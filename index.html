// HOW TO PLAY

  • 2 players alternate turns placing O or X on grid intersections.
  • Pieces connect when adjacent horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  • To score: form a complete closed loop of your pieces. Enemy pieces trapped inside are captured as points.
  • Empty cells inside a loop are not scored — only enemy pieces count.
  • The player with the most points when no moves remain wins.
